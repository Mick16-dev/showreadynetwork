How it works

  1. 01

    You tell me what you run

    You send genre, city, room size, how often you promote. I need the actual picture — not a deck, not a follower count. I don't add you to a database or ask you to "onboard."

  2. 02

    I match when there's a fit

    When a brand wants a room like yours, I make one introduction — both sides get context upfront. No mass email, no list rental, no vague "partnership opportunity" blast.

  3. 03

    You deal direct

    You and the brand take it from there. Creative, deliverables, the room. You keep your list.

Who's behind this

About

Show Ready Network is me, Michael. I'm not from the promoter side of the room. My background is biochemistry and metabolic engineering. In that work you learn fast that systems fail when the handoffs between people are broken. I kept seeing the same pattern in events: promoters who can't find the right brand partners, and brands who can't find promoters they actually trust.

I started Show Ready because that disconnect keeps good people stuck. Not for lack of talent, but for lack of a clear path through the noise. This isn't a platform or an agency. It's a small bridge. You tell me what you run or what you're looking for, I read it myself, and I make an intro when the fit is real.

I'm based in Bremen, building across Europe. Founding roster of twelve on purpose. Small enough that every name means something.
