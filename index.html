Sequence

How it works

  1. 01

    You tell me what you run

    You send genre, city, room size, how often you promote. I need the actual picture — not a deck, not a follower count. I don't add you to a database or ask you to "onboard."

  2. 02

    I match when there's a fit

    When a brand wants a room like yours, I make one introduction — both sides get context upfront. No mass email, no list rental, no vague "partnership opportunity" blast.

  3. 03

    You deal direct

    Money, creative, deliverables — between you and the brand. You keep your list and your terms. I don't take a cut unless we agree that in writing first.

Who's behind this

About

Show Ready Network is me — Michael. I've spent the last several years around independent nights in the UK and Europe: booking rooms, chasing riders, arguing about load-in times. Club shows, live bills, warehouse rooms. Not agency work. Not a platform pitch.

I started this because I kept seeing the same gap from both sides — promoters with real crowds who couldn't get a straight sponsor conversation, and brands who wanted a room but didn't know who to trust. Someone had to make the introduction properly.

I'm keeping the founding roster at twelve on purpose. If you're a brand reading this, that means names you can actually look up — not a directory of five hundred profiles.
